About Us

Born from the hearts of Saamarth Agarwal and Sukhman, Dilmure is a snack brand with a mission to redefine healthy snacking in India without compromising on fun or flavor. We believe that clean, honest ingredients don’t have to be boring. That’s why we’ve crafted a range of millet-based munchies that are bold in taste and kind to your body.

At Dilmure, we say a firm no to MSG, maltodextrin, preservatives, trans fats, cholesterol, and palm oil. Our snacks are made to be light on the stomach, rich in flavor, and suitable for everyone from curious kids to mindful elders.

Rooted in Indian culture and powered by global wellness trends, our products are gluten-free, guilt-free, and full of personality. Whether you're reaching for a quick bite or replacing a full meal, Dilmure brings you a crunchy, flavorful experience you can feel good about.
